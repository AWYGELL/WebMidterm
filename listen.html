Listen The World


Welcome to a world of exciting adventures, fascinating facts, and captivating stories designed just for you! Our engaging podcasts are like a magical gateway to learning and fun, all rolled into one. In this section, you'll discover a treasure trove of audio gems that will make you want to explore and ask questions about the incredible world around you. So, grab your headphones and get ready for a fantastic voyage into the realm of knowledge and imagination!

Podcasts Collection

'The Ten News'

In just 10 minutes or less, "The Ten News" show is here to make current events super interesting for kids! It covers important topics like who gets to be on the Supreme Court, how the mail gets delivered, those big debates where people run for president, why there might not be enough people to help with voting, and even why girls and boys might not always get paid the same in sports.

'KidNuz'

You can listen to a new episode every weekday morning, and it's just seven minutes long. They talk about things like what's happening in politics, cool science stuff, exciting sports news, and fun entertainment stories. At the end of each report, they have a little quiz to see if you were really paying attention.

'The Big Fib'

Each week on "The Big Fib," a super cool TV show made just for kids between 7 and 12, you'll meet L.I.S.A. the robot and Deborah Goldstein, who's like the leader of the show. They bring in a special kid to the show, and then two grown-ups show up, both saying they know a ton about something. But guess what? One of them is telling a big fib! It's up to the kid to listen carefully, ask clever questions, and use their super smart thinking to figure out who's for real and who's just pretending.

'What If World'

Imagine a magical world where anything is possible! What If World is a super fun podcast made just for kids. It's all about exploring wild and crazy ideas. What if a teeny-tiny dragon was your closet buddy? What if you could eat ice cream forever and ever? And what if mischievous cats were in charge of everything? Join the adventure with Abacus P Grumbler, Randall Radbot, and Whendiana Joan as they help Mr. Eric craft amazing stories inspired by your questions!
